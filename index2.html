The Fayence market

The Fayence market is held regularly on Tuesdays, Thursdays and staurdays. the stands are normally deployed at the latest from 8am, and the merchants begin to tidy up from 12:30am. It's located on Place Thiers, also called Place de l'Eglise Saint-Jean Baptiste. It's to the east, above the city center. Merchants can be found at around 90 stalls. It sells: fruits, vegetables, products from local producers, clothing, ustensils, furniture, toys or other non-food products
