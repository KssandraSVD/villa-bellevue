Rules of life

For the smooth running of your stay, and that of the people who will follow you, we thank you in advance for not smoking inside and for respecting the few points below.

  • For their safety, never let your young children wander unsupervised.
  • For hygienic reasons, pets are not allowed.
  • Ladies, it is IMPERATIVE to NEVER flush sanitary pads or tampons down the toilet.
    The pipes are absolutely not intended for this purpose.

We are aware that it is not very pleasant to be told what seems obvious to the proper maintenance of a living space, unfortunately from experience, we know that it is sometimes necessary to remember this.
The garbage containers are located down the road which leads to the villa next to the tennis courts. Please close the rental access gate after each passage as well as the windows and doors when you are away.
